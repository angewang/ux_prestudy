Welcome to Prime!

Welcome to Prime! During the next 18 weeks you will gain experience in the entire UX lifecycle: from research, through prototyping, design, evaluation, and development. You’ll spend time working through the process of identifying goals, prototyping solutions, and evaluating how well those solutions meet the goals. We’re going to give you a taste of this complete process in Prework, introducing some design principles, methods, and tools used by professionals.

The goal of Prework is to ensure that everyone comes to the classroom speaking a common design language. For some of you, there may be content that is familiar while for others this may all be new to you. In any case, we expect you to fully engage with the content, with your peers, and with your instructors. This is a team based learning environment; each of you have something to bring to the group as a whole.

As a UX designer, you will be focused on crafting experiences that are both usable and meaningful for the intended users. You will also be responsible for a basic understanding the business context that your design will operate within and the technical constraints that make it functional and reliable. We will start to understand what this takes in these next six weeks, and will dig even deeper once you’re in the classroom.
